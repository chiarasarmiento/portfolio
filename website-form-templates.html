Website Form Templates

30+ templated online forms now being used on numerous WordPress websites across the United States and the Philippines.


I was assigned with creating a new iteration of templated forms at my previous company. I worked on a team and had the opportunity to redesign both the visual layout and the functionalities to align with the approved design.

Design

The color scheme chosen for the design of the forms is deliberately kept simple to ensure compatibility with any website.

Functionalities

The older version of forms featured basic form validation. These are the new functionalities integrated into the forms:

  • Name:
    Now restricts input to alphabetic characters, excluding numbers and special characters.
  • Social Security Number:
    No longer permits special characters in the input.
  • Date of Birth:
    The date picker disables selection of future dates.
  • Phone Number:
    Now accommodates the selection of a country code.
  • Salary:
    Now automatically formats the input in the correct currency format.
  • Upload:
    Now accepts only doc, docx, and pdf file formats, with a maximum file size of 10 MB.
